2022 Philippine Presidential Election

scheduled to be held on Monday, May 9, 2022,
as part of that year's general election.
This will be the 17th direct presidential election and 16th vice presidential election in the Philippines since 1935,
and the seventh sextennial presidential and vice presidential election since 1986.

1987 Philippine Constitution

Incumbent president Rodrigo Duterte is ineligible for re-election as he is limited to a single term,
under the 1987 Philippine Constitution. The position of president and vice president are elected separately;
the two winning candidates could thus come from different political parties.

Philippine Constitution

According to the Constitution of the Philippines of 1987,
the election is held every six years after 1992, on the second Monday of May. The incumbent president is term-limited.
The incumbent vice president may run for two consecutive terms.

Official Candidates

The Commission on Elections is expected to publish a tentative list of candidates by late October 2021.
The commission will have finalized a list of candidates that would appear on the ballot by December 15, 2021.
